Apricot Chocolate Icecream

Ingredients:

  • 1-1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 5 tsp unsweetened Dutch-process cocoa powder
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 2 cups fresh (or frozen) ripe apricots, pitted and chopped

Procedure:

  • Combine cream, cocoa powder and sugar in large saucepan set over medium-high heat,
    whisking until mixture comes to full rolling boil and starts to foam.
  • Remove from heat and stir in apricots.
  • Cover and let stand 10 minutes to cool.
  • Puree mixture in food processor or blender, working in batches if necessary.
  • Refrigerate puree, covered, at least 4 hours or overnight.
